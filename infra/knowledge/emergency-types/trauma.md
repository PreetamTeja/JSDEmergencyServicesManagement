# Trauma

**Trigger phrases:** accident, road accident, fell down, fracture, broken
bone, bleeding, crush injury, hit by vehicle, knocked down, injured, cut,
wound, head injury, collision, motorcycle accident.

**Mass casualty upgrade:** bomb blast, building collapse, explosion, stampede,
multiple injured → severity Critical, patients = stated number.

**Plant context:** industrial trauma (machinery, molten metal, chemical burn)
at [loc-gate](../locations/loc-gate.md) or [loc-workshop](../locations/loc-workshop.md)
should be treated as Critical by default.
