# Cardiac

**Trigger phrases:** heart attack, chest pain, chest tightness, heart, cardiac
arrest, not breathing, stopped breathing, collapsed, unconscious, no pulse,
palpitations, he fell down not moving.

**Notes:** Default to Critical — time-sensitive, every minute counts.
If caller says "just chest pain, he's conscious" → Urgent is acceptable.
Nearest hospital with cardiac ICU is [TMH](../locations/loc-tmh.md).
