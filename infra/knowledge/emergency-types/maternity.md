# Maternity

**Trigger phrases:** labour, in labour, delivery, pregnant, baby coming,
contractions, water broke, she's about to give birth, pregnancy complication,
bleeding during pregnancy, premature.

**Notes:** Always Urgent minimum. If baby is imminent ("crowning", "can see
the head") → Critical. Destination: [TMH](../locations/loc-tmh.md) maternity
ward.
