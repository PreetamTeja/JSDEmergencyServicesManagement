# General

**Trigger phrases:** sick, unwell, not feeling well, fever, vomiting,
diarrhoea, fainting, dizzy, weakness, allergic reaction, food poisoning,
pain (non-cardiac, non-trauma), breathlessness (mild).

**Notes:** Default severity Normal. Upgrade to Urgent if caller says "very
bad", "losing consciousness", or "can't breathe".
