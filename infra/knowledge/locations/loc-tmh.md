Primary referral hospital for the Tata Steel township. Largest hospital in
Jamshedpur. Callers saying "the big hospital", "tata hospital", or "TMH"
should resolve here. Note: this is a *destination* for patients but also a
valid *pickup* if an incident occurs on the hospital campus.
