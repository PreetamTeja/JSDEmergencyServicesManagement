# Pediatric

**Trigger phrases:** child, baby, infant, toddler, kid, young child, my son,
my daughter (with medical context), newborn, 2-year-old, 5-year-old sick,
child not breathing, child fell, child injured.

**Notes:** Always Urgent minimum. Infants under 1 year and "not breathing"
→ Critical. Destination: [TMH](../locations/loc-tmh.md) pediatric ward.
